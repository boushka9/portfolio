About Me

I have a love for learning that runs deep. Nothing beats the feeling of finally understanding how something works or unraveling a complex problem. In my work, I strive to be detail-oriented, like when you're putting together a puzzle. I believe that paying attention to the small things is what ultimately leads to a successful and satisfying outcome, with the big picture serving as a guiding force.

When I'm not working, you can often find me outside, enjoying a walk with my furry companion, Scout. I'm a proud dog parent and love spending my time with my pup. Another passion of mine is building with LEGO's. I have an extensive collection that I proudly display, and I find the process of constructing a LEGO creation to be both relaxing and stimulating.

Overall, I'm someone who values curiosity, attention to detail, and finding joy in simple pleasures. I'm always eager to learn and explore new things, and I hope to bring that same enthusiasm to everything I do.
